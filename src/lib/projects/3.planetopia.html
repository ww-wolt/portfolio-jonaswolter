Museum Exhibition

For the current temporary exhibition at the Museum of Communication, Berne, I designed and programmed a dozen touch screen terminals with a quiz on sustainability and subsequent evaluation. Based on their answers, visitors could sign a suitable world change agreement at the final stage. I also designed and programmed software for detailed anonymous live exhibition data evaluation, which provided the marketing department and museum management with valuable new insights into visitor behaviour.
